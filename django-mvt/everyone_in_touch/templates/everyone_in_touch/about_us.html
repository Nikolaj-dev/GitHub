What is Everyone in Touch?

Everyone in Touch is a service that helps clients and professionals meet.

Clients find professionals here to learn any language(perhaps any language) - English, Spanish, French etc. In total, there are more than 2,300,000 specialists in Everyone in Touch on 100+ languages.

Private specialists find clients with the help of EIT. More than 20 thousand new orders appear on our website every day.

How can a client choose a specialist?

Enter on the site in the search bar the specialist or service you need and answer a few questions - when to start work, what budget do you expect, where is it convenient to meet.

The order will appear on the site, specialists will offer their services and prices. Choose the right one: by reviews, rating, examples of work. Pay attention to the mark "Passport verified" in the application form. The search for a specialist is free. And for the work done, you pay the specialist directly in a convenient way. the work you pay is guaranteed in the original way.

How can a specialist find an order?

All orders are visible in your account. Choose the one that suits your budget, timing, location. Send a response - an offer to the client of your services. With the response, the client will receive your phone number and will be able to contact you.

Several specialists may respond to an order. The client chooses the right one.
At EIT, you pay per completed order or per response, depending on your specialty.
After the work is completed, you will receive the money directly from the client, the entire amount will remain with you.

How do reviews appear on the EIT site?

As soon as the specialist has confirmed that the order has been completed, we will ask the client to leave feedback on his work.
All reviews on the site are real: only a person who has found a specialist through the EIT website can publish a review.

Who is responsible for the result?

We do not provide services, but help specialists and clients to meet. It is important for us that the service we create is reliable and secure.

What we do:
  • We ask all specialists to pass the verification of documents. Those who did this are easy to find by the mark “Passport checked” in the profile;
  • We publish only verified reviews. With the help of reviews, customers can form an impression about a specialist;
  • We block specialists and clients who violate the rules of the service;
  • We help to deal with difficult situations. If you have any difficulties while working, please contact our support team at nikolajpetrishchev@gmail.com.
What we recommend to you:
  • check the documents of the person with whom you will work. Write down the data of the passport and registration;
  • if the specialist you chose did not come to you, refuse his services and let us know about it;
  • draw up a contract before starting work. This will ensure that both parties understand the task and conditions correctly;
  • if you transfer money or valuables to a specialist, draw up an agreement;
  • choose specialists who give a guarantee. Warranty conditions are usually specified in the profile.
